üéØ Choosing the Right Format

Decision Tree

‚ùì Ask Yourself:
  • Who is the audience? - Technical team vs. business stakeholders
  • How much detail is needed? - Quick overview vs. comprehensive specification
  • What's the timeline? - Immediate vs. planned implementation
  • Are there compliance requirements? - Enterprise policies vs. informal process
